Sightseeing

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Other popular sightseeing activities include:

  • Boat or bus tours of the island
  • Hikes through the tropical rainforest
  • Visits to the active volcano
  • Local history museum
  • Helicopter rides over the island
  • Snorkeling along the coast

Entertainment

Many entertainment options are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

  • Several pubs including a microbrewery
  • A new dance club
  • Movie theater
  • Arcade
  • Art galleries
  • Bowling alley
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest
  • Nine-hole golf course (opening soon)
